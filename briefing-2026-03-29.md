**Lesson 5 of 12: The Two-Agent Pattern**

Stop trying to do everything in one conversation. Use an **Initializer agent** to create a structured plan once — a checklist with clear pass/fail criteria — then a **Worker agent** that reads the plan every session, does one task, and logs progress before stopping. This pattern keeps work on track across sessions without losing context or repeating setup.
